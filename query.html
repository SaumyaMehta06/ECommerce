Security & Payment Issues

Safe and Secure Shopping Is it safe to use my credit/debit card on Flipkart? Your online transaction on Flipkart is secure with the highest levels of transaction security currently available on the Internet. Flipkart uses 256-bit encryption technology to protect your card information while securely transmitting it to the respective banks for payment processing. All credit card and debit card payments on Flipkart are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways managed by leading banks.
